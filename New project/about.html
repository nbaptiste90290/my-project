The Ace International School

At The Ace International School, Ghana, we are committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive learning environment for our
students. With a focus on academic excellence, character development, and fostering a love for learning, we strive to prepare our
students for success in the 21st century and beyond.


Our dedicated team of highly qualified teaching and non-teaching staff is passionate about education and works tirelessly to create
engaging and innovative lessons that cater to the individual needs of each student. We believe in a holistic approach to education,
where students are encouraged to explore their interests and develop critical thinking skills.



The Ace International School’s curriculum is enriched with key aspects of the British Cambridge Curriculum. As a school
dedicated to ensuring the best outcomes for all students, we believe that pupils in our educational setting should have the right
to a transformational educational experience that will enable them to fulfill their potential and realize their ambitions. This
fantastic enrichment program focuses on Maths, English, and Science, providing our students with the opportunity to transition
easily into schools that offer the Cambridge Assessment International Examinations and different pathways in their educational
journey towards a sustainable and bright future.


Our spacious and serene environment provides students with the resources they need to thrive academically. From well-equipped
classrooms to science and computer labs, we ensure that our students have access to the latest technology and tools to enhance
their learning experience.




At The Ace International School, we understand the importance of extracurricular activities in a well-rounded education. We offer
a wide range of clubs, sports teams, and arts programs to cater to the diverse interests of our students. Whether it’s joining the
robotics club, playing on the basketball team, or participating in the school drama troupe, there is something for everyone to get
involved in.


We also prioritize the well-being and social-emotional development of our students. Our dedicated counseling team provides
support and guidance, fostering a safe and inclusive environment where they can thrive both academically and personally.


As a family-oriented school, we value parent involvement and believe in the power of collaboration between home and school.
We encourage parents to actively participate in their child’s education through regular communication, parent-teacher management
conferences, and volunteering opportunities.


But don’t just take our word for it—here’s what some of our students and parents have to say about their experience
at The Ace International School:


- “The Ace International School has provided my child with a nurturing and challenging environment. The teachers truly care
about their students’ success and go above and beyond to help them achieve their goals.” - Parent



- “I love how The Ace International School encourages us to explore our passions. I’ve had the opportunity to join the debate
club and discover my love for public speaking.” - Student






So, whether you’re a parent looking for the perfect school for your child or a student seeking an enriching educational experience,
we invite you to explore all that The Ace International School has to offer. Join us on this exciting journey of learning, growth,
and discovery!
