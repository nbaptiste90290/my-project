our Academics


Our Programs

At The Ace International School, we offer a wide range of academic programs designed to meet the needs of all learners.
Our programs include Early Years, Primary School, and Secondary School education following the British Cambridge
Curriculum.


Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum emphasizes academic excellence and holistic development. Core subjects include;

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science and
  • ICT
    • along with a variety of extracurricular activities and enrichment programs.


Faculty and Staff

Our highly qualified and dedicated teaching staff are committed to delivering engaging
and innovative lessons tailored to each student's learning style and abilities.


Facilities

Our modern facilities
include well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, a library,
and sports fields to provide students with an excellent learning environment.


Academic Resources

  • Library with extensive academic materials
  • Science laboratories for hands-on learning
  • Computer labs with up-to-date technology
  • Access to Cambridge Assessment materials

Admissions

Join our vibrant academic community.
Admissions are open throughout the year for qualified students.
